# Receiver Wiring Details

## Components
- Microcontroller (Arduino/ESP32)
- Photodiode or LDR
- 10k ohm resistor (pull-down)

## Example Connections
- Photodiode -> analog pin (e.g., A0)
- 10k ohm resistor -> pull-down to GND
- Common ground between board and receiver circuit
